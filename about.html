Our Story

Crafted With Heart Since 2005

From humble beginnings to a celebrated dining destination, Grilli is rooted in community, craft, and hospitality.

Who We Are

Every Flavor Tells A Chapter

Grilli was founded by friends who believed that a meal could be both comforting and daring. Today, our team of chefs, farmers, and artisans collaborate closely to honor ingredients at their peak.

We blend global culinary inspiration with local partnerships, ensuring that every plate reflects a balance of innovation and warmth. The dining room is our stage, and our guests are always the stars of the evening.

  • Fresh Ingredients

    Daily deliveries from trusted farmers, fisheries, and foragers.

  • Expert Craft

    A kitchen brigade with Michelin-level experience across three continents.

  • Sustainable Focus

    We reduce waste, repurpose ingredients, and champion ethical sourcing.
